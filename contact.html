Intermediate

This involves creating a user-friendly conversational interface that can guide the user through the chatbot experience. The conversational flow should be designed to gather relevant information from the user, provide relevant responses, and offer actionable advice or recommendations.

working process

It responds to questions posed to it in natural language as if it were a real person. It responds using a combination of pre-programmed scripts and machine learning algorithms. When asked a question, the chatbot will answer using the knowledge database that is currently available to it.

Objective

Serve as 24/7 companions, monitor health status in real-time, and automatically call for assistance in case of an emergency. Help manage chronic conditions, mental health issues, and behavioral and psychological disorders.
